I am Ticao Zhang.

I got a bachelor and master degree in Electrical Engineering in Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China in 2014 and 2017, respectively. On 16 Aug 2017, I started my PhD program in Auburn University, where I was adversied by Prof. Mao.

My research interest lies in video signal processing, optimization in wireless networks and machine learning.

Research

Metadata-Reduction for Soft Video Delivery
Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Networking Letters, vol 1, no 2, pp 84-88, June 2019.

We proposed a blind data detection method that recovers the video signals from the squared amplitude of the received signals. This method is almost metadata free.
An Overview of Emerging Video Coding Standards

ACM GetMobile, vol.22, no.4, pp.13-20, Dec. 2018.

we provide an overview of existing and emerging video coding standards.
Cooperative Caching for Scalable Video Transmissions over Heterogeneous Networks
Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Networking Letters, vol.1, no.2, pp.63-67, June 2019.

We investigated a cooperative video caching problem in a heterogeneous networks(HetNet).
Smart Power Control for Quality-driven Multi-user Video Transmissions: A Deep Reinforcement Learning Approach
Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Access Journal, Special Section on Mobile Multimedia: Methodology and Applications, vol.8, no.1, pp.611-622, Jan. 2020. DOI: 10.1109/ACCESS.2019.2961914.

We proposed a deep reinforcement learning based power control method.
Energy-efficient power control in wireless networks with spatial deep neural networks
Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Transactions on Cognitive Communications and Networking, Special Issue on Evolution of Cognitive Radio to AI-enabled Radio and Networks, to appear. DOI: 10.1109/TCCN.2019.2945774.

We show that it is possible to bypass the complex channel estimation process and directly perform power control with GLI when the channel state information (CSI) can be viewed as a function of distance dependent path-loss.
Joint power and channel resource optimization in soft multi-view video delivery
Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Access Journal, Special Section on Mobile Multimedia: Methodology and Applications, vol.7, no.1, pp.148084-148097, Dec. 2019. DOI: 10.1109/ACCESS.2019.2946607.

we proposed efficient resource (bandwidth and power) allocation algorithms. Simulations results demonstrate that the proposed distortion-resource (DR) optimization algorithm can ensure a best viewing quality under a resource constraint and the proposed resource-distortion (RD) optimization algorithm can minimize the resource usage for a target video quality requirement.
