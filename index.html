I am Ticao Zhang.

I got a bachelor and master degree in Electrical Engineering in Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China in 2014 and 2017, respectively. On 16 Aug 2017, I started my PhD program in Auburn University, where I was adversied by Prof. Mao.

Research

Metadata-Reduction for Soft Video Delivery
Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Networking Letters, vol 1, no 2, pp 84-88, June 2019.

We proposed a blind data detection method that recovers the video signals from the squared amplitude of the received signals. This method is almost metadata free.

Ticao Zhang, Shiwen Mao
IEEE Networking Letters, vol.1, no.2, pp.63-67, June 2019.
